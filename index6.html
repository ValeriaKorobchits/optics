Contacts

To find the perfect lenses for yourself you need to follow these simple tips:

  • Your contact lenses should solve the problem that drives you to wear the lenses. Your lenses should provide good vision by correcting your nearsightedness, farsightedness, astigmatism, or some combination of these vision problems.
  • The lens must fit your eye. For this, lenses have tens of thousands of combinations of diameter and curvature. Of course, not every brand of lens is represented in every "size".
  • Your doctor is able to evaluate the physiology of your eye and your vision to determine which lens best meets the above two criteria.
  • You may have another medical need that affects your lens choice. For example, your eye doctor may choose a particular lens if your eyes tend to be dry, injured, or have other eye problems.
  • Once you and your eye doctor have chosen the right lenses for you, you will be issued a contact lens prescription. You can purchase a supply of lenses from our store.
